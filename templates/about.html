About the Project

The project involved the development of a blogging website, for the end users to share and communicate with others in a social environment delivered over the internet. The website development was a challenging task that included building of web application, designing it's architecture and interface, hosting the application and establishment of domain name suited for the purpose.

The main objective was to craft the website to deliver rich-user experience and seamless operation and processing. Therefore, consistent efforts were laid out by our team to accomplish the objective and design a worthy blogging platform.
